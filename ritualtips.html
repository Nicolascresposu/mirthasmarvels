Best sacrificial meals
For when long pig doesn't cut it

For meat-based demons and most hellbound underlings, flesh is the only ingredient in their diets, more often than not, these demons express a preference in some animals, or some preparation of the flesh; The Louisiana jazz devil historically only ever accepts elder goose meat, however, there is one chop that will be accepted by any demon; A prime mutton from a newborn goat.
